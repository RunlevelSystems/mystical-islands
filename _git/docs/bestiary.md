# Birdy

## Creature Type
Wildlife Avian

## Threat Level
Low

## Habitat Regions
[Aetherfall Isle](islands.md#aetherfall-isle), [Sylvanreach Isle](islands.md#sylvanreach-isle), [Galestone Isle](islands.md#galestone-isle)

## Description
Skittish island bird often seen before storms and predator movement.

## Combat Style
Non-combat scavenger that triggers nearby enemies if startled.

## Behavior
Flocks around roads, docks, and ruins; disperses when fighting starts.

## Abilities
Alert calls, short glide bursts, scavenger peck.

## Weaknesses
Fragile and easily scattered by noise.

## Loot & Resources
Feathers, light meat, alchemy oils.

## Encounter Design
Used as ambient ecology and early warning wildlife.

## Regional Placement
Primary in starter roads and forest clearings; secondary on cliff coasts.

## Lore Notes
Sailors treat sudden silent flocks as an omen of nearby monsters.

### Variants
- Elite
- Corrupted
- Ancient
- Venomous
- Armored
- Undead
- Alpha
- Boss
- Elemental
- Diseased
