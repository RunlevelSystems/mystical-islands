# 🗺 Islands Overview
## Overview
The archipelago is split into six major regions: Mainland, Witchwood, Frostpeak, Ashen Deadlands, Shattered Reefs, and Stormreach.

## Gameplay Purpose
Each island represents a different gameplay identity and progression challenge.

## Player Experience Goals
- regional variety
- faction-driven travel decisions
- escalating environmental risk

## Technical Notes
Island modules are built to support additive world expansion.

## Implementation Notes
MVP ships with Mainland as primary playable zone and references other islands through hooks and rumors.

## Examples
- Frostpeak = crafting metallurgy identity
- Shattered Reefs = pirate trade and naval risk

## Production Priority
**P0 for Mainland, P1-P3 for additional regions.**
