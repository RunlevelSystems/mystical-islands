# 🌍 Lore Index

This section captures the mythic foundation of Mystical Isles: its history, factions, cultures, classes, political tensions, and the long-term vision that gives every system narrative weight.

## What This Section Covers
Use these pages to understand the truth of the archipelago, the stories players encounter, and the worldbuilding that frames progression, conflict, and discovery.

## Included Documents

### [🏝 World Overview](world_overview.md)
Mystical Isles is a fractured archipelago where old-world technology, magical mutation, and frontier civilization collide.

### [📖 Fractured History](fractured_history.md)
The Fracturing was a civilization-ending collapse caused by unsafe dimensional energy exploitation.

### [True History](true_history.md)
Mystical Isles is the aftermath of a technological civilization collapsing under its own dimensional engineering.

### [🗺 Islands Overview](islands_overview.md)
The archipelago is split into six major regions: Mainland, Witchwood, Frostpeak, Ashen Deadlands, Shattered Reefs, and Stormreach.

### [🏴 Factions Overview](factions_overview.md)
Major powers include Crown Compact, Thornbound Circles, Moonroot Enclaves, Frostpeak Holds, Cinder Tribes, Gravebound Legions, Free Reef Corsairs, Reef...

### [🏰 Political System](political_system.md)
Politics in Mystical Isles is an emergent layer built on faction influence, settlement control, route security, and resource leverage.

### [🧬 Races and Classes Overview](races_and_classes.md)
Race and class define early identity, but long-term expression comes from skill specialization, faction ties, and gear choices.

### [Player Lore](player_lore.md)
Sailors still call the archipelago the **Shattered Crown Isles**, though no two charts agree on where the old crown truly fell.

### [📜 Developer Vision](developer_vision.md)
Mystical Isles is inspired by classic MMORPG eras where community, danger, and discovery mattered more than checklist speed.
